FAQ

Does Verbum AI work fully offline?

Yes. Verbum AI runs on-device, so you can summarize documents without uploading them to the cloud.

Do you upload my files to a server?

No. Your documents stay on your device. Verbum AI is designed for privacy-first workflows.

How large of a PDF or a document can Verbum AI summarize?

Verbum AI is built for long documents and has been tested with PDFs and documents up to 860 pages. Performance depends on your device and document type (text vs scanned).

Which platforms are supported?

macOS and Windows.
